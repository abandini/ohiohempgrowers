About Us

Who We Are

The Ohio Hemp Growers Association was founded in 2019 following the legalization of hemp cultivation in Ohio. We are a non-profit organization comprised of farmers, agricultural experts, processors, and industry advocates dedicated to establishing hemp as a viable and sustainable crop in Ohio.

Our organization brings together stakeholders from all aspects of the hemp industry to share knowledge, advocate for fair regulations, and promote the growth of this versatile crop throughout the state.

With members from all regions of Ohio, we represent a diverse community of agriculturalists united by their interest in hemp's potential to diversify farm income, support sustainable farming practices, and develop new economic opportunities across the state.

Our Vision

We envision Ohio as a leader in sustainable hemp production, where farmers thrive by growing high-quality hemp for diverse markets, from fiber and grain to CBD and other applications. We believe hemp can play a vital role in Ohio's agricultural future by providing farmers with new revenue streams while supporting environmental sustainability.

Our vision includes:

  • A robust hemp industry in Ohio that spans the entire supply chain from seed to sale
  • Sensible regulations that protect consumers while encouraging industry growth
  • Research-backed best practices that enable successful hemp cultivation
  • Strong markets for all types of hemp products
  • Recognition of hemp as a standard rotational crop in Ohio agriculture

Our Achievements

2019

Founded After Hemp Legalization

Established the organization following the passage of Ohio Senate Bill 57, which legalized hemp cultivation in the state.

2020

First Annual Conference

Hosted our inaugural hemp conference with over 200 participants from across Ohio.

2021

Regulatory Advisory Committee

Established a formal role advising the Ohio Department of Agriculture on hemp regulations.

2022

Research Partnership

Launched hemp variety trials in partnership with Ohio State University Extension.

2023

Member Growth

Reached 250 active members representing every region of Ohio.

2024

Market Development Initiative

Created connections between Ohio growers and processors, facilitating over $2 million in contracts.

2025

Comprehensive Resource Library

Launched expanded online resources and educational materials for members.
