Membership Benefits

Why Join Our Association

The Ohio Hemp Growers Association is a community of farmers, entrepreneurs, and advocates dedicated to advancing the hemp industry in Ohio. By becoming a member, you gain access to valuable resources, networking opportunities, and a collective voice in shaping the future of hemp in our state.

Whether you're an experienced hemp farmer or just getting started, our association provides the support, education, and connections you need to succeed in this growing industry.

Membership Benefits

Exclusive Resources

Access our comprehensive library of guides, templates, and research papers on hemp cultivation, processing, and marketing.

Networking

Connect with fellow growers, processors, retailers, and industry experts through member-only events and our online community.

Education

Attend workshops, webinars, and field days focused on best practices, new technologies, and industry trends.

Advocacy

Gain representation at the state and federal level as we work to shape policies that support the hemp industry.

Industry Updates

Receive regular newsletters with market insights, regulatory updates, and upcoming opportunities.

Market Access

Get connected to buyers, processors, and retailers looking for quality Ohio-grown hemp products.

Discounts

Enjoy member discounts on events, services, and products from our industry partners.

Expert Support

Get answers to your questions through our member support line and expert advisory network.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does membership last?

Membership is valid for one year from the date of joining. You'll receive a renewal notice approximately one month before your membership expires.

Can I upgrade my membership level later?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership at any time. The difference in membership fees will be prorated based on the remaining time in your current membership period.

Do I need to be actively growing hemp to join?

No, we welcome individuals and businesses at all stages of involvement with the hemp industry, including those who are still in the planning or research phase.

How can I get involved beyond basic membership?

Members can volunteer for committees, contribute to our newsletter, speak at events, or participate in our mentorship program. Contact us after joining to express your interest in these opportunities.

Is my membership fee tax-deductible?

Membership fees may be tax-deductible as a business expense. Please consult with your tax advisor for guidance specific to your situation.
