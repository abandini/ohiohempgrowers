Frequently Asked Questions

When can I apply for a hemp growing license in Ohio?

The application window runs from November 1 to March 31 each year through the Ohio Department of Agriculture.

What is the minimum acreage required to grow hemp in Ohio?

You must have at least 1/4 acre for outdoor cultivation or 1,000 square feet for indoor cultivation.

How much does a hemp license cost?

The application fee is $100, and the license fee is $500 per growing location.

What happens if my hemp tests above 0.3% THC?

If your crop tests above the 0.3% THC limit, you may request one additional test. If it still exceeds the limit, the crop must be destroyed.

Where can I purchase hemp seeds or starts?

We maintain a list of reputable suppliers for our members. It's important to source from suppliers who can provide documentation of seed genetics and THC compliance.
