Annual Conference

Ohio Hemp Conference 2025

August 15-16, 2025
Columbus Convention Center
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Join us for our annual conference featuring industry experts, educational sessions, equipment demonstrations, and networking opportunities. This year's theme is "Cultivating Success: The Next Phase of Ohio's Hemp Industry."

Conference Highlights:

  • Keynote speakers from across the hemp industry
  • Panel discussions on regulatory developments and market trends
  • Breakout sessions on cultivation techniques, processing, and marketing
  • Equipment and supplier exhibition hall
  • Networking events and opportunities to connect with buyers and processors
  • Research poster presentations from Ohio universities

Registration Information:

Early Bird (until June 15): $175 members / $250 non-members

Regular (June 16 - July 31): $225 members / $300 non-members

Late Registration (August 1-14): $275 members / $350 non-members
